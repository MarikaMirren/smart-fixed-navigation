The idea of the project started out as a reading response to one of the chapters from the book The Way the World Works by Nicholson Baker. The chapter I read was about war. Nicholson discusses Pacifism and World War II events in this chapter. My main challenge was to come up with a project that would reflect the idea in the reading and also make it my own. On the model of the recent war in Georgia that happened in 2008 I decided to use the event as a source to reflect the general war situation around the world. For more Information and research visit the blog languageofwar.tumblr.com
